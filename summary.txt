THE HYDRATION NARC — MORTAL SINS REGISTRY
==========================================
A complete catalogue of every act of violence the Narc can and will commit
against you, in ascending order of severity.

─────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 1 — ATMOSPHERIC HOSTILITY
─────────────────────────────────────────

  [1] VERBAL SHAMING (any threshold)
      The macOS "Daniel" voice reads context-appropriate insults aloud.
      Non-blocking. Daniel is always available. Daniel never sleeps.

  [2] SCREEN DIMMING (score ≤ 60)
      Display brightness dropped to 20% via osascript.
      Restored after 0.5 seconds. A preview of what's coming.

  [3] MOUSE JITTER (score ≤ 60)
      Cursor teleports to 5 random positions within a 40px radius.
      Returns to origin. Achieves nothing except reminding you who's
      in charge.

─────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 2 — COLLATERAL DAMAGE
─────────────────────────────────────────

  [4] APP HOSTAGE (score ≤ 30)
      Detects frontmost application. If it is Spotify, Discord, or YouTube,
      the app is hidden (not closed — hidden). Still running. Unreachable.
      Voice line: "No music for raisins. Drink up."

  [5] KEYBOARD SEIZURE (score ≤ 15)
      A pynput Listener with suppress=True intercepts every keystroke.
      All keyboard input silently discarded.
      Voice line: "Your words are worthless. Only water matters now.
                   Keyboard privileges revoked."
      Unlocked by: Smile + Sip (verified by FaceMesh landmark ratio ≥ 0.28).

─────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 3 — SOCIETAL CONSEQUENCES
─────────────────────────────────────────

  [6] SLACK WEBHOOK ALERT (score = 0)
      HTTP POST to configured webhook URL. Notifies your channel:
      "DEHYDRATION ALERT: [User] has ignored me for 10 minutes.
       They are officially a raisin."
      Voice line: "I'm telling your coworkers you're dying of thirst.
                   Goodnight."

  [7] GRUMPY SIP PENALTY (sip without smile)
      Smile intensity (mouth-corner / face-width ratio) below 0.28
      at time of sip. Health recovers only to max(current, 50) instead
      of 100. Voice line: "You look miserable. Smile while you drink
      or it doesn't count."

─────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 4 — THE NUCLEAR OPTIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

  [8] FORCED SLEEP — THE NUCLEAR PENALTY (score = 0 for 60 seconds)
      osascript puts the entire machine to sleep.
      Voice line: "Your failure has been recorded. The ledger does not lie."
      Logged to ledger.json as a "mortal_sin" with UTC timestamp.

  [9] THE RICKROLL (wake from sleep + no sip within 30 seconds)
      Browser opens: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
      No voice line. The music speaks for itself.

─────────────────────────────────────────
TIER 5 — BUREAUCRATIC HUMILIATION
─────────────────────────────────────────

  [10] THE UNSTOPPABLE CLAUSE (> 3 mortal sins in one day)
       stop.sh refuses to kill the process.
       Output: "You haven't earned the right to stop. Keep drinking."
       Manual override: kill -9 $(cat .narc.pid)

  [11] THE PUBLIC CONFESSION (negative Purity Score)
       shame_report.py calculates (sips × 10) − (mortal_sins × 50).
       If negative, generates a confession string and opens
       x.com/intent/tweet with the text pre-filled. You must click Post.
       The Narc considers the manual click part of the punishment.

─────────────────────────────────────────
SCORING REFERENCE
─────────────────────────────────────────

  Purity Score = (Sips × 10) − (Mortal Sins × 50)
  Raisin %     = min(100, Mortal Sins × 20)

  A perfect day:  10 sips, 0 sins → +100 (you are a person)
  A bad day:       2 sips, 3 sins → −130 (you are a raisin)
  The worst day:   0 sips, 5 sins → −250 (you are a cautionary tale)
